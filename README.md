## 💡 What is ComplyPanda?

ComplyPanda is an **open-source AI compliance assistant** that helps compliance officers, legal teams, and fintech companies navigate the complex world of Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Counter-Terrorist Financing (CFT) regulations.

Built with cutting-edge AI technology, ComplyPanda provides **instant, accurate answers** to compliance questions in both **Italian and English**, drawing from a curated knowledge base of official regulatory documents.

### 🎯 The Problem

Compliance professionals spend hours searching through hundreds of pages of regulations, directives, and guidance documents. Finding the right answer quickly is hard.

### ✨ The Solution

Ask Panda. Get instant, sourced answers with references to official regulations. Simple.

## 🌟 Key Features

### For Compliance Professionals

- ✅ **Regulatory Research** - Instant answers on FATF, 5AMLD, 6AMLD, MiCA
- ✅ **Italian Compliance** - Deep knowledge of D.Lgs 231/2007, UIF, Banca d'Italia
- ✅ **KYC/CDD Guidance** - Customer due diligence procedures and best practices
- ✅ **Sanctions Screening** - PEP lists, sanctions compliance, Travel Rule
- ✅ **Report Generation** - Export conversations as Markdown for documentation

### Technical Excellence

- ⚡ **Edge Runtime** - Deployed on Vercel Edge for global low latency
- 🤖 **Groq AI** - Powered by Llama 3.3 70B (fastest inference in the world)
- 💾 **Smart Caching** - Instant responses for repeated queries
- 🎯 **Intent Recognition** - AI understands context and intent
- 📊 **Full-Text Search** - PostgreSQL-powered knowledge base
